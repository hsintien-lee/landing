About Us

We serve students and young adults who are looking for an affordable, medium-to-small scale moving service. Our service, “Pivot”, is a platform similar to Uber, which allows you to book a “moving buddy” and vehicle to assist you with transporting furniture and other items from one apartment to another. Customers will have the opportunity to choose between vehicles of a variety of different sizes, anywhere from a pickup truck to a large moving van. The benefit to the users is a cost-efficient and helpful alternative to hiring a truck through one of the current moving companies, all of which overcharge for smaller moves.
